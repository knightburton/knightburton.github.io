About me

My name is Imre Kiss.
Ever since I was a kid playing with computer games and I have been interested with technology.
My interest began with a Commodore 64 computer which has a huge impress on me (I still really love the 8bit stuffs).

A few years later I took every oportunity to improove my knowledge.
I chose the IT line when I had to choose - I mean every single time!

and after that I've graduated on the University of Szeged as Software Engineering.
Besides, I dealt a lot of physical equipments - I mean programmable tools - like PIC, boards, sensors, etc...

I really really enjoy creating something new for everybody on the other side of the screen.
