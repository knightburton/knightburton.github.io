About me

My name is Imre Kiss. Ever since I was a kid playing with computer games and I have been interested with technology.

My interest began with a Commodore 64 computer which has a huge impress on me (I still really love the 8bit stuffs). A few years later I took every oportunity to improove my knowledge. I chose the IT line when I had to choose - I mean every single time! I studied in an IT and technical oriented high school, I finished an Internet Application Developer course and I study in the University of Szeged, Department of Software Engineering right now. Besides, I dealt a lot of physical equipments - I mean programmable tools - like PIC, boards, sensors, etc...

A few people asked me "Why are you doing this?", my answer is shocked them - because they thought my answer will be the money - but in fact I really really enjoy creating something new for everybody on the other side of the screen.

In the other hand I'm not a fully introverted geek guy.
I'm doing a lot of things IRL.
